Brand Summit Meeting

I was asked to design sinage and handouts for a branding meeting attended by Maytag, Whirlpool, KitchenAid, and Amana. The theme for this meeting was: Think Differently. This included clean two-dimensional designs that fit with the current trends the client was describing in the meeting. Another aspect was to show a "techy" side hense the motherboard and lightbulb. With the cube the idea was that you could solve the puzzle normally or you can tThink Differently and solve it another way.

For this project I focused on consulting with the client and drafting the ideas before developing any concept fully. This way I as able to know exactly what the client wanted and the intentions behind each decision.
